The Journey

I'm a Product & New Media Designer currently transforming digital experiences at Air India DesignLAB. My work serves 450+ daily users across operations, crew management, and passenger systems.

With a Master's from the National Institute of Design (NID) and 4+ years of experience, I specialize in design systems that scale, data visualizations that inform, and interfaces that adapt. I believe the best digital products feel alive — they breathe, remember, and evolve with their users.

Beyond design, I explore consciousness through technology. My installations have investigated ego dissolution, dream-to-meaning engines, and the intersection of AI with human perception. I'm fascinated by how interfaces can become more than tools — they can become partners in human creativity.

When not designing, you'll find me reading Douglas Hofstadter, playing Baldur's Gate 3, or prototyping the next generation of conscious interfaces.
